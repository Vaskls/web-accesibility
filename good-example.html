Coffee · Cats · Calm

A quiet café where cats live and you visit

Purrfect is a cat café in the city centre. We have 8 resident cats and a full coffee menu. You can sit, drink, and spend time with the cats. No loud music. No crowded rush hours.

All our cats are rescue animals. They live here permanently. Visits are calm and at your own pace.

About the café

We opened in 2021. The café has two quiet rooms and an outdoor terrace. The cats move freely. You do not need to interact with them — they will come to you if they want to.

We ask visitors not to wake sleeping cats, not to use flash photography, and to keep voices calm. This keeps the environment peaceful for both cats and people.
